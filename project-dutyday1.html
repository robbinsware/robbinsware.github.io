Duty Day Calculators

SnapShot

  This app was developed to help aircrews calculate all applicable duty day restrictions to help simplify caculations for crews hauling stuff aroudn the globe for Uncle Sam.

Initial Deployment

 Lauched in 2014, from the Tillman USO Center .

Supported Platforms

 iPhone & iPad  (sorry no Android)

Description

  Duty Day Calculator for crews flying around the globe for Uncle Sam. This app will quickly help you calculate all applicable duty day restrictions in both Zulu and Local time zones. Simply enter the Alert, Show or Takeoff time and all Vol-3 restrictions will be instantly calculated for you. No more frustrating calculus at the end of your duty day while TACC is trying to sell you on another "good idea".
